 Traveling within Bangalore:

  • From Airport: Please take the air-conditioned Volvo airport bus BIAS-8, and get off at Electronics City, Infosys campus (last stop). The journey is about 1.5 to 2 hours (depending on traffic), and the fee about Rs 200/- ($4). There are places to keep your luggage on the bus.

  • From City Center Majestic: Please take the air-conditioned Volvo airport bus 356C, and get off at Electronics City, Infosys campus (last stop). The journey is about 1 to 1.5 hours (depending on traffic), and the fee about Rs 40/- ($1). There are no places for luggage on these buses.

  • From other parts of Bangalore: Go to Majestic via the nearest Volvo stop (there are many in the city).

Other Modes of Transport: You can also hire taxicabs, with fee varying between Rs500 to Rs1000/-.
