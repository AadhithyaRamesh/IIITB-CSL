One of Professor Venkatachaliegar’s favorites was ‘Ramanujan’s remarkable summation formula’ for which he found several applications and gave elegant, self contained proofs. More applications have since been discovered periodically. Also, as is well known, the remarkable formula has, as its special cases, many classical formulas of seminal importance in the theory of q-series and number theory. In this talk, I wish to present some more, what seem to be, new and interesting applications of the remarkable formula.
