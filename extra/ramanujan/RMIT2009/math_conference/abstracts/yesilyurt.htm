In a handwritten manuscript published with his lost notebook, Ramanujan stated without proofs forty identities for the Rogers-Ramanujan functions.
Most of the elementary proofs given  for these identities are based on Schroter-type theta function identities in particular, the identities of L.J.Rogers. We give a generalization of Rogers's identity. Applications to modular equations,Ramanujan's identities for the Rogers-Ramanujan functions as well as new identities for these functions are given.
