Ramanujan's Contributions to Theta Functions

 Perhaps Ramanujan devoted more research time to theta functions, including modular equations and number theory related to theta functions, than to any other topic.  Since in the past five years mock theta functions have been in the forefront of research arising from Ramanujan's mathematics, and since Ramanujan's contributions to this subject were an outgrowth of his earlier work on theta functions, it perhaps would be wise to survey Ramanujan's work on theta functions at this time.
