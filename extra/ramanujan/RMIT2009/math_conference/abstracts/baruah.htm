New hypergeometric-like series for 1/π2 arising from Eisenstein series and Ramanujan’s Cubic Theory of Elliptic Functions

In his famous paper, “Modular Equations and Approximations to π,” Ramanujan recorded 17 hypergeometric-like series representations for 1/π. It was not until 1987 that proofs of all 17 series representations for 1/π were found by Jonathan and Peter Borwein. These authors and several other authors in the past two decades have found many new series for 1/π as well as for 1/π2. Recently, the speaker and his co-authors B. C. Berndt and N. Nayak used certain representations for Eisenstein series and Ramanujan’s classical and cubic theories of elliptic functions to derive many new hypergeometric-like series representations for 1/π2.
