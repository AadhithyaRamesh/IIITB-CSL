Analogues of Ramanujan's partition congruences.

In his paper, "Some properties of p(n), the number of partitions of n", Ramanujan gave two proofs of his famous partition congruences for primes 5 and 7. In his second proof, he used two exact partition identities. We shall describe how analogues of Ramanujan's exact identities may be obtained. (Joint work with Heng Huat Chan.)
