The Rank and Crank of Partitions - In Memory of A.O.L. Atkin

 In this talk we examine the problem of exact linear relations for the rank and the crank of partitions as well as the problem of congruence relations. This talk is in memory of A.O.L. Atkin and highlights some of his work. The first linear relations were found for the rank mod 5 and 7 by Dyson (1944) although the mod 5 results are encoded in an identity in Ramanujan's Lost Notebook. The first congruence relations for the rank were found by Atkin and Hussain (1958). Congruence relation for the crank of partitions are related to a PDE connecting the generating functions of the rank and the crank due to Atkin and Garvan (2003). We also discuss new congruences for the rank and the crank of partitions.
