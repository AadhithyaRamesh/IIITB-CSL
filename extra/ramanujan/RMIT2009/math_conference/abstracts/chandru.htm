The algorithmics of folding proteins

The protein folding problem is a well known challenge problem in modern biology. It involves predicting the three dimensional structure from the sequence information, by minimising some energy function. Because of the difficulty of the general problem, researchers have studied folding in discrete lattice models. Even these much simpler abstractions have proved to be very hard. We survey the results on the computational intractability of folding in lattice models and some approximation algorithms for folding in these models.
