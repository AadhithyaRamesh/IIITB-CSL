  • Talk 1 : Ramanujan's mathematics - some glimpses
    Prof. B. Sury, ISI
    Abstract:
    Whenever, the name of Srinivasa Ramanujan is mentioned, it usually stops with a discussion of his romantic story. But it is rare to find a discussion of details of his mathematics. It is a fact that not much effort has been made by mathematicians familiar with Ramanujan's works, and this is partly because Ramanujan's mathematics is difficult. Here, we make a modest effort to bring glimpses of Ramanujan's mathematics to a reasonable level.
