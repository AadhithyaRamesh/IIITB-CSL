  • Prof. B. Sury (b. August 21, 1960; New Delhi)


    joined the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research, Bombay in 1981 after completing his Master's in Mathematics from I.I.T.Delhi. His Ph.D. thesis `Algebraic groups and discontinuous subgroups' was written under the supervision of Professor M.S.Raghunathan, FRS. He was in TIFR from 1981 until 1999. Due to his interest in teaching undergraduates, he moved to the Indian Statistical Institute in Bangalore where the B.Math.Hons. Programme started. He is at present a Professor of Mathematics in ISI Bangalore. He was an editor of Resonance, a journal of science education published by the Indian Academy of Sciences from 1999 to 2005. He has also been involved in the mathematics olympiad programme since 1993. At present, he is the Karnataka Regional co-ordinator for the Indian National Mathematics Olympiad. He has written two books `The Congruence Subgroup Problem' and `Group theory - selected problems'. His areas of research are algebra and number theory. He was the mathematician member of the editorial board which brought out the newsletter during the International Congress of Mathematicians, Hyderabad in August 2010.
