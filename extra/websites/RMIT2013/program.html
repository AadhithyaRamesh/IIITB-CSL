    10:00 AM - 11:00 AM
    		 Prof. Kalyan Chakraborty
    "Preventing unknown key-share attack using cryptographic bilinear maps."
    Abstract: We consider a couple of well-known two-pass protocols and show that
    these protocols are not unknown key-share attack resilient. Finally we
    describe a couple of three-pass protocols derived from the above protocols
    by adding an extra pass which provides additional key confirmation and
    prevents unknown key share attack.
