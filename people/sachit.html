    Sachit Rao
    Ph.D (Ohio State University), CV


    Sachit Rao obtained his Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Ohio State University (OSU), Columbus, USA; B.E., Mechanical Engineering ,University of Mysore, and M.S., Mechanical Engineering , OSU . Prior to joining IIIT-B, he worked at Electronics and Energy Conversion Lab, G.E. Global Research—Bangalore, India as Summer Intern , Department of Mechanical Engineering—OSU as Graduate Teaching Assistant , Institute of Automatic Control Engineering, Technische Universitaet Muenchen (TUM)— Munich, Germany , Department of Aerospace Engineering, IISc—Bangalore, India as Centenary Post-Doctoral Research Associate , Systemantics India Pvt Ltd—Bangalore, India as Senior Engineer–Research , NextFirst Engineering Technologies Private Limited—Bangalore, India as Consultant, International Institute for Aerospace Engineering and Management, Jain University— Bangalore, India as Assistant Professor.
